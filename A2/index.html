Day by day.

The line graph on the right is divided into two parts: one for categoric cases and the other for number of countries.

Note: hover around for more details


The graph of categoric cases is a clear picture of how the outbreak escalated. Up until 20th March, the line stays low as the rise in cases was steady.

Subsequently, the line graph increases exponentially. The virus aggressively spread out in such a short time. On the bright side, recoveries soar relatively higher than deaths and active cases mostly due to precautions taken and intensive medical assistance.

We can see that the number of countries infected were small up until around March. Most of the countries became infected from there onwards.

IsGlobal (2020) states that its main tranmission path is by the air. Many countries contracted the virus because of travels and movements which were too late to stop in the early stages.

However, with more awareness and decreased outdoor activities, deaths and active cases were kept on a slow increase.
